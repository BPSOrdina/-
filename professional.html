The Ordina BPS Accelerator Program focuses on professionals that have been in the business for several years, and now want to take their skills to the next level. We can provide you with a solid one month training programme that lifts your career to an expert level.

Coach & Inspire

IT Best Practices

Business Processes

We believe that knowledge about business processes is vital in an ever changing IT and customer environment. We will help you to approach the organization you will be working in and for in an efficient and effective way.

Security & Privacy

Knowledge and application of security and privacy principles have become mandatory in the IT consultant curriculum of today. Training programs with certification such as CIPT and OWASP are only 2 examples of possible development tracks.

Softskill

Agility

Agility is becoming more and more a strategic weapon in markets that are characterized by volatility, uncertainty and change. Innovation needs to happen in increasingly shorter cycles. Product development must be driven by the impact that is made on users and customers. Organizations need to react quickly on rapidly changing circumstances. Training programs with a proven track record such as Scrum will help you to be prepared.

Development

Security & Identity

Security and identity are two ingredients that are at the heart of everything we do. During this track we will deliver a solid foundation of all identity components that are used in enterprise environments, both on-premises and in the (Microsoft) Cloud. As environments can grow quickly, governance and reporting are indispensable parts of a mature management process.

We will take you on a journey, unfolding all details about federated identities, single sign-on and Multi-Factor Authentication. This will give you a solid understanding of authentication & authorization mechanisms.

Are you looking for guidelines on how to write secure code or implement SSL-enabled solutions? Our experts will provide you with the latest tips and tricks, right from the field, to further enhance your security awareness.

Softskill

Technical efficiency

As a productivity expert we have to lead by example, during this course we will increase our efficiency by automating our work. Making use of PowerShell we will be able to script repetitive tasks, mitigate human errors and make our life easier.

Are you living on the code side of life? No problem, we will also provide you with the latest insights on how to optimize team development efforts by using best practices from the DevOPS field.

Join this track if you want to master your technical efficiency skills in a world where automation will become your best friend.

Softskill

Hybrid Architecture

Migrations of different services towards the cloud is ongoing at a very high rate. Although cloud adoption is only happening faster, a lot of organizations still (partially) depend on their on-premises infrastructure.

In this track you will learn to master every aspect of a hybrid configuration. Identifying requirements, moving mailboxes, sites and unified messaging accounts between on-premises and the cloud, and managing both cloud- and on-premises infrastructure in one holistic approach.

Using the knowledge of on-premises product variants as a stepping stone, we gain deep insights into hybrid configuration of Exchange Server, Skype for Business Server and SharePoint Server.

Integrating all of the above, you will also get familiar with implementing and supporting a Microsoft Groups and Teams setup.

Architecture

Development

Office 365 introduces many new opportunities in terms of development. Plugging into the cloud platform API, new technologies and possibilities are unlocked. Along with all those possibilities come new tools and frameworks to develop lightweight solutions.

During our development track we will use virtual development environments like Docker to practice and enhance our client-side scripting skills. Leveraging the modern platforms, toolsets and frameworks like Azure, O365, Angular, React and many others, we will develop new innovative applications and plugins.

Architecture

Automation

Cloud is all about automation; from deploy to run automation is used to optimize the usage of infrastructure and deployment costs. During this course you will learn the necessary skills to automate tasks using scripting languages like PowerShell, bash or python. You will get to learn tools like ansible, chef or puppet.

Architecture

Devops

Application delivery from a infrastructure point of view are changing. Container technology is on the rise and managing this environment requires other skills then a classic server landscape. During this course you will learn more about managing Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, Pivotal Cloud Foundry, OpenStack and OpenShift.

Development

Platform & storage

Hardware infrastructure today comes in many forms ranging from a classical virtualization platform with a central storage system over hyper converged system to the cloud. In this track you will learn all these different platforms like VMware, Hyper-V, DellEMC storage solutions, VXrail, Azure stack, AWS and Azure.

Development & Operations

Networking

At the heart of every business environment is the network that interconnects everything. Connecting users in a secure manner, over a physical connection or wifi, with servers and the internet all has it different challenges. In the software defined datacenter, the network is not without its own challenges in the software defined networking space. In this track you will learn about networking, switching and routing, network access control (NAC), firewalling, load balancing, VPN and many more concepts.
